strenght of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are
several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19
vaccine work :

  • Avoid alcoholic bberages

  • Avoid stress

    Stress has a profound effect on immune system work.

    production and oxidative stress in the body

  • Eat healthy food

  • Get enough sleep

  • Exercise or physical activity
